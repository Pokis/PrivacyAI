FAQ

Is my data private?

Yes. All AI processing happens locally on your device using Chrome's built-in Nano Gemini model. No text is sent to the cloud.

Why "Writer" vs "Chat"?

Chat is for conversation. Writer is optimized for generating long-form content like emails or stories without conversational filler.

What is "Editor" mode?

Editor allows you to paste text and have the AI rewrite it to be more formal, casual, or shorter.

Why does it say "Not Supported"?

You probably need to enable Chrome Flags. Check the "Setup Required" section or click "Help".
